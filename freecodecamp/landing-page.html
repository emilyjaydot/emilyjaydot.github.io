Frequently Asked Questions

How long do balloon rides last?

Plan for 3.5 hours from the time we meet and check-in, safety-brief, inflation, flight, landing, retrieval, post-flight toast, and return to your car. The flight portion will last at least one hour. The memories last a lifetime.

How can I book a balloon ride?

Click the button above for the package that you want to book, and enter the requested information. It takes most people less than 4 minutes to guarantee their place! Be sure to check your inbox for the confirmation email and details (don't forget to look in your Spam folder).
